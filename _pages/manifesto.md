_Preface: My manifesto is my forceful commitment; my constant source of inspiration. I strive to live by it every day._

Never stop learning.

Try new things.

Ask questions.

Seize opportunities.

Get out of your comfort zone.

Travel often.

Achieve mastery.

Help people.

Be kind.

Observe.

Smile; it helps.

Declutter; life is simple.

Be brave.
